Ardi

Ardi is a quick and capable companion for crafting custom elements. Ardi's philosophy is to go back to the basics and #usetheplatform without any Virtual DOM, JSX, compilers, or any other 'magic' to provide the most performant un-framework available, all without sacrificing DX. Ardi is tiny but fierce, weighing in at just 1kb uncompressed.
